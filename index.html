What is the CPS Test?

The CPS Test (Clicks Per Second Test) is an online tool designed to measure how many times you can click your mouse button within a specific time frame. It's a simple yet effective way to benchmark your clicking speed and track your improvement over time.

Originally popularized by the Minecraft community, CPS testing has grown into a widespread practice among gamers who play titles requiring rapid mouse clicking.

üí° Did You Know? The average person clicks at around 6-7 CPS, while professional gamers can achieve 10-15 CPS or even higher using advanced techniques.

Why Do People Take CPS Tests?

  • Gaming Performance: Higher CPS provides advantages in games like Minecraft, Roblox, and Cookie Clicker
  • Competition: Gamers compete to achieve the highest CPS scores on leaderboards
  • Skill Development: Tracking CPS helps measure improvement in clicking techniques
  • Fun & Challenge: Many people enjoy testing their limits and beating personal records
  • Mouse Testing: CPS tests help evaluate mouse switch responsiveness

How to Test Your CPS

Testing your clicks per second is straightforward. Here's how to get accurate results:

  1. Choose Your Duration: Select a test duration. The 5-second test is most popular, while 10-second tests provide more accurate averages.
  2. Position Your Hand: Place your clicking finger comfortably on the mouse button. Relax your hand‚Äîtension can slow you down.
  3. Start the Test: Click the start button and immediately begin clicking as fast as possible.
  4. Maintain Consistency: Try to keep a steady rhythm rather than burning out quickly.
  5. Review Results: Check your total clicks, CPS score, and rating. Take multiple tests for accuracy.

‚úÖ Pro Tip: Take at least 3-5 tests and calculate your average. Single tests can be affected by lucky or unlucky streaks.
